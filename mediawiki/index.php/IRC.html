      IRC

      From 57North Hacklab

      Our IRC channel can be found at #57N on irc.57north.org.uk (Freenode).

      IRC Clients

      Many IRC clients are available:

      • Weechat
      • irssi

      If you're a hackerspace member and you use another client, please help your fellow hackers by contributing a little documentation.

      Rules

      1. IRC is a real-time medium and users will not expect their contributions to be recorded forever. Please do not publish logs from the channel. Small excerpts may be alright, but please ask permission from those who contributed before publishing them.
      2. Strive to be a catalyst.

      Bots

      HackerdeenBot

      HackerdeenBot is the 57North IRC bot, built by Ormiret. You can issue commands to the bot for information about the hacklab.

      hackercat

      hackercat is the 57North IRC bot, built originally by irl but now maintained by Derecho. You can issue commands to the bot for information about the weather and to search YouTube. hackercat also archives all links posted to the page to [1].
