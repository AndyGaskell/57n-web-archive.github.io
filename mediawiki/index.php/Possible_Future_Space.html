      Overview

      While we are not currently or directly looking for a new location, it is always good to keep an eye on locations options and opportunities.

      25/08/19 Update: Actually, we kinda are: See archive email

      This page is intended to be treated only as background research and not as any plan or intent - just a crowd sourced brainstorm thing. It may however serve as the information source for a move decision at some point.

      General notes...

      • The Engage space is around 37 sq.m / 400 sq.ft, the Union St space was perhaps 20 sq.m / 215 sq.ft.
      • An ideal budget would be around ~£4000 p/a, as that is what we've typically been able to sustain.
      • All affordable places will be below the threshold for paying business rates


      Arbitrary rating

      It seemed like a good idea to have a sort of scoring scheme to evaluate places relative to each other, based on a set of criteria. Something like...

      Arbitrary rating:

      • Geographical location:
      • Amount of space:
      • Suitability of space:
      • Cost:
      • Landlord arrangement:
      • Inital work required:
      • Total: N out of 30

      ...out of five.

      Some of these values can be less arbitrary, like...

      Cost:

      • 1 star: £600 -> £800
      • 2 star: £400 -> £600
      • 3 star: £200 -> £400
      • 4 star: £0 -> £200
      • 5 star: free

      Amount of space:

      • 1 star: 0 -> 15 m2
      • 2 star: 15 -> 30 m2
      • 3 star: 30 -> 45 m2
      • 4 star: 45 -> 60 m2
      • 5 star: 60 + m2

      3 Thistle Lane, Aberdeen

      Added: 15 July 19, by AG

      Smart Self Contained West End Office building on Thistle Lane, Aberdeen is new to the market and now available to rent. New Decor and carpet fitted, quiet location in city centre with parking permit avail from ACC, Overlooks a Garden. Must be seen, Viewings avail now.

      Internally the property comprises entrance lobby (about 1.5m x 1.5m), office (5.85m x 2.7m) and single WC (about 1.5m x 2m).

      20.05 sq.m / 216 sq.ft

      Cost: £300 p/m (£3,600 p/a)

      https://www.gumtree.com/p/commercial-property-to-rent/self-contained-office-studio-on-thistle-lane-aberdeen.-/1340070294

      Note from Andy: I went to view this on the 28th of August and was shown round by Cliff from Letts & Co. Some notes from that viewing...

      • The space is good in that it's one big room, that would be flexible in terms of dividing it up into zones, but it is not so big, at (5.85m x 2.7m, 15.795 m2). IN practive this is not much bigger than the main room in the Engage space (5.7m x 2.6m) but it feels a lot bigger I guess because it is lighter and less cluttered.
      • The place is in good condition, with new-ish paint, good lighting and a new carpet.
      • It is nice and light, but still quite private, with most of the windows facing east overlooking the garden of the neighbouring tenement.
      • There are around 4 electric heaters
      • The windows are double glazed
      • There are plenty of plug sockets, also a phone socket
      • It is below the threshold for rates, so the only bills would be electricity and phone/internet
      • It is owned by the Letts & Co, they have at least one other non-profit tenant and sounded like they'd be a fair and reliable landlord.
      • We can get one, maybe two, resident's parking permits from ACC which I think are £40 p/a
      • Although there are adjoining buildings, I think it's just a garage and a shed, so little risk of noise complaints
      • It is good in terms of disabled access, as far as I can tell, from my limited knowledge of physical accessibility best practice. There is only a small step to enter the space, not sure if the WC entrance is wide enough for a wheelchair.
