      Zero-Waste Shopping

      From 57North Hacklab

      Shopping with zero-waste is a good thing, but it can be hard to find places to shop to buy the things you want to buy. The following is a list of places that are known to be either zero-waste by default, or at least zero-waste friendly, in Aberdeen City.

      Contents

      • 1 City Center
        • 1.1 Food Story Cafe
      • 2 Northfield
        • 2.1 Dennis Paterson Butchers
      • 3 Wishlist

      City Center

      Food Story Cafe

      Our mission is to reduce as much plastic and carbon emission we release into our environment and with our zero waste refill stations you can help us reach that goal. Our shop offers you a range of items, from cleaning products to organic vegetables as well as our homemade wholemeal breads baked 6 days a week.

      More info...

      • http://foodstorycafe.co.uk/our-shop/

      Northfield

      Dennis Paterson Butchers

      Good selection of meat products, will happily place items into lunchboxes or whatever container you bring instead of using plastic bags. 

      5, Lintmill Terrace,
Aberdeen, Aberdeen City, AB16 7SR
