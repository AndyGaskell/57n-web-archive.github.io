      EngageSpace

      From 57North Hacklab

      For our new space to be workable we need to build like the little doozers that we are, have a look through the pages below pick an area and get involved, we need hands and gumption.

      A good thing to do is think what you would like to space to provide for you and try and guide the build to provide those things, we need ideas, you can also see what others are doing and help them build the parts of the space they require.

      The areas of work are separated below into type of work, location and functional items, Have a look though the type of work you are interested in and contact the people in charge using the maillist, this will help us organise the work so it meets the members needs.

      Physical Power Lighting Network Access Control
      Main Room Workshop Room Upstairs Room Stair Space Exterior
      Tools Furniture

      There is also include a snagging list on our pad, for people to quickly add problems in the new space which should be resolved, keep these short, it is generally for things which do not fit into any of the above sections.

      Timeline

      The timeline is just a guide and may not be what actually happens.

      May

      May is primarily closeout of 35a Union St - empty it, tidy it.

      May 1st: Notice at 35a Union St. Meeting w/ membership if desired.

      May 4th: Agreement in place with Engage Gaming.

      May 11th: Compile a list of questions and observations points we need to look at for each different section of the build

      May 12th: Meet Sheena to Inventory Space at 1pm. Pack goods for moving afterwards.

      May 13th: Move All retained space equipment to Engage.

      May 19/20: Moving all retained space equipment to Engage.

      May 22: Clean and Tidy 35a Union St

      May 29: Design Meeting for all @ 35a Union St, Clean & Tidy V2.0, final goodbyes.

      May 31: Handover of Keys.

      June

      In June, we build our new space together. Tuesday nights become building nights, and I'm sure people will come in for a day or so at the weekends too!

      June 5: Tuesday Buildout Meeting: All hands on deck, let's get building our new space. Fitout and Furniture. Shelving and sawing.

      June 12: Tuesday Buildout Meeting: Power and Lighting with added network wiring

      June 19: Tuesday Buildout Meeting: Power and Lighting with added network wiring part 2.

      June 26: Clean & tidy, see what needs to be finished out.

      July 3: Open Tuesday #1 in Engage Space.
